My Interests

Music plays a major role in keeping myself free from stress and relaxes me more than anything else. It even motivates me during hectic days. There is no specific genre that I like, but listening to K-Pop boy band BTS's music helps me stay joyful and motivated. Arts and Crafts

Pencil sketching is something I got so interested about after seeing my friend doing it. I got some help from her about how she do it and tried it out myself. I tried my Brother's portrait and it came out well for a beginner. So, I continued sketching a lot more and gave it out those as gifts for some of them. My sketches has been included to the gallery section of last page. I have also did the Craft works like Explosion box, photo cube and other DIY and gave them out as gifts. I did craft work during the lockdown period out of boredom. It helped me to make crafts out of waste materials that were in house. I enjoy doing arts and crafts work while listening to some music.

Drama and Webseries

Drama is something I started without knowing anything about it. Me and my friends started doing drama for a event conducted at my college. We started it from the scratch, right from story to executing a whole play on stage. We got so interested after doing our first play and did upto 4 dramas. Most of our stories were inspired from short stories. Crime-thriller, Time travel, Anthology and Psychic thriller were the genre we took as the main plot of the plays we did. After COVID-19 I was not able to continue doing plays on-stage but I still enjoy watching it. Apart from all other Hobbies, I'm interested in watching web series of any language. I mostly watch English, Korean and Tamil web series. I personally prefer Thriller and Sit-coms. I enjoy watching the TV series Friends and The vampire diaries a lot. I watch an episode daily as a relaxation.
